Academic & Technical Pathways

We offer two distinct pathways, thoughtfully designed to guide students at their own pace through their Ordinary and Advanced Levels.

General Education

A well-rounded academic curriculum that builds foundational knowledge for the GCE Ordinary and Advanced Levels.

  • Mathematics
  • English Language
  • French
  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology
  • Economics
  • Geography
  • History
  • Religious Studies
  • Pure Mathematics
  • Further Mathematics
  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology
  • English Literature
  • Geography
  • Economics
  • History

Technical Education

Practical, hands-on skills training from Forms 1 to 3, preparing students for self-reliance and further technical studies.

  • Automobile Repair Mechanicsm(ARM)
  • Electrical Power System (EPS)
  • Fashion Design (FE)
  • Building Construction (CE-BC)
  • Home Economics (HEc)

Commercial Education

Valuable business and commerce courses for Forms 1 to 4, equipping students with modern workplace skills.

  • Accounting
  • Commerce
  • Office Practice
  • Shorthand
  • Computer Studies

15 Sep

2026/2027 Registration

We are delighted to welcome new students for Forms 2, 3, 4, and Lower Sixth. Registration for the 2026/2027 academic year is joyfully open until September 30th.

  • Venue: SJCCC Campus
  • Time: 9:00 AM – 12 noon (Tue–Fri)
  • Fee: 2,000 FCFA
  • Required Documents: Photocopy of birth certificate, recent progress report card, and a transfer certificate from the previous school.

Please contact the Principal Office with any questions.

22 Oct

Open Day & Career Fair

Come take a stroll through our classrooms, workshops, and laboratories. Meet our friendly faculty and explore the career paths available in our General and Technical fields.

  • Time: 9:00 AM – 4:00 PM
  • Special Highlights: Live welding & fabrication demos, science lab experiments.
  • Who Can Attend: Prospective students, parents, and community members are all welcome.
  • Refreshments: A light lunch will be provided.

No prior registration needed — we can't wait to meet you!

Form One Entrance Interview

We warmly invite all prospective Form One students to join us for a placement interview. This is a lovely opportunity for us to get to know your child and ensure we are the perfect fit for their journey.

  • Closing Date: Wed, 30th Sep 2026
