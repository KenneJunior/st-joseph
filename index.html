15 Sep

2026/2027 Orientation & Registration

New and returning students are invited to our orientation programme. Registration for the 2026/2027 academic year runs until 30th September.

  • Venue: SJCCC Main Hall
  • Time: 8:00 AM – 3:00 PM (Mon–Fri)
  • Registration Fee: 5,000 FCFA (non‑refundable)
  • Required Documents: Birth certificate, last school report, 2 passport photos

Contact the Registrar's office for late registration queries.

GCE Mock Examinations

Final preparatory exams for Ordinary and Advanced Level candidates. Timetables will be distributed by class teachers one week prior.

  • Duration: 5th – 19th December 2026
  • Subjects: All GCE Ordinary and Advanced Level subjects
  • Late‑evening Study: Supervised sessions from 6:00 PM – 8:00 PM (optional)
  • Materials Needed: Drawing instruments, calculators, and ID cards

Hard copies of the timetable will be posted on school notice boards.
